What I do

I practice a methodical and disciplined approach to problem solving; iterating tirelessly over various stages of the process described below

User Research

Conduct user interviews, ethnographic field studies, and market research in order to visualize the user's journey, identify their pain points and establish a foundation upon which an experience can be built

Strategy

Build upon foundational research and pre-existing user mental models to create a framework for content and features to be used across all stages of the experience

Prototyping

Explore a multitude of interaction and layout patterns at varying levels of fidelity that will empower the user to seamlessly traverse through different stages of the experience

Testing

Validate (or invalidate) design decisions by placing prototypes in the hands of real users, gauge their reactions to the proposed solution, and use those insights to further iterate until a refined solution is achieved
