About me

I'm a UX designer from Chicago with a passion for studying human behavior, writing scalable software, and competitive Indian dance. I employ extensive user research in my design process to get to the core of users' frustrations. I ask my users for magic wand solutions, and use that to conceptualize the impossible.

I began my digital career as a front end developer, writing software to pay the bills while learning about the product development process. I used the analytical skills and process knowledge I gained as a developer to turn concepts into minimal viable solutions.

I also moonlit as a choreographer and dance instructor for a dance team. Throughout my years as a performer, I learned a great deal about crafting memorable experiences. Today, I'm powered by the idea of designing for a wide digital audience.

I work methodically and occasionally get sidetracked by articles on behavioral economics.

Contact me to learn what I can do for you.
