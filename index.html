What I do

I practice a methodical and disciplined approach to problem solving, iterating constantly over various stages of the process described below

Understand

Conduct user and stakeholder interviews, ethnographic field studies, and market research in order to understand the client's goals and user's needs

Define

Identify the user's pain points and define the specific problem meant to be solved

Strategize

Build upon foundational research and user mental models to create a framework for content and features across all stages of the experience

Prototype

Explore a multitude of interaction and layout patterns at varying levels of fidelity that will empower the user to seamlessly traverse the experience

Iterate

Validate (or invalidate) design decisions by placing prototypes in the hands of real users, gauge their reactions and use those insights to further iterate to a refined solution

Deliver

Present the final solution in a timely manner via the appropriate medium; whether it be through a presentation, workshop, design spec document or front-end code.
